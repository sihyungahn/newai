Benefits of Lunges You may not think of lunges as targeting your abs, but these exercises are very effective all-over body toners, especially for your “core” muscles. Stand with your feet hip-width apart, then step forward into a lunge position. Make sure to keep your back knee about 3 inches off the ground. You can add small dumbbells for a little more intensity.
