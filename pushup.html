What is Squat???

Building the muscles of the front of the thigh Exercise area Thigh Exercise period 1 day Exercise time 20 minutes Fitness equipment Barbell, machine Number of program iterations 4 times The front thigh muscle building is an exercise program that trains the quadriceps muscle, known as the strongest muscle in the body.

The front of the thigh exercise means that if you exercise only the front, the effect of the exercise decreases, so the front and the rear are simultaneously performed. Squat, which is the most basic lower body exercise-Barbell, leg curls that stimulate the back of the thigh-Lying, lunge that improves balance in the front of the thigh-Barbell, leg curls that strengthen the back of the thigh-Standing, five leg extensions that develop quadriceps.

Squats Help Build Muscle. They don't just help you achieve wonderful, toned legs; they promote body-wide muscle building by creating an anabolic (muscle building) environment in the body.
